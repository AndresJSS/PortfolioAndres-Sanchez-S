About me

Welcome to my portfolio website! Beginning my adventure into the world of machine learning and data science! Previously, I obtained my degree in Biosystems Engineering and then I have been working as a researcher in different projects. Continuously learning with open mind and positive attitude.

  • Winter 2017 - 2018

    Michigan State University Study Abroad

    International Study Abroad: Ecological Engineering in the Tropics. A series of tours were made in Costa Rica, learning about different agri-ecological production systems, analyzing them since an energy perspective. As a final project, a novel solar biopower system was implemented as a low emission and high efficiency waste treatment technology.

  • April - May 2019

    23rd International Seaweed Symposium (ISS) 2019

    "Exploring solar radiation relations of seaweed floating at sea: a tool to counteract ocean warming?"(OR 15-1, pag.122 Abstract Book). We analyzed solar radiation relations of satellite images of Sargassum seaweed masses from the Caribbean/Gulf of Mexico coasts.

  •

    October 2019

    Ansys Convergence Mexico 2019 (16th edition)

    "Modelling and simulation of a novel concentrated solar radiation receiver for small-Fresnel collector power plant using Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)". To increase anaerobic biodigesters efficiency is possible to build biomass-solar radiation hybrid systems, avoiding energy feedback problems.

  •

    November 2019

    Latin American & Caribbean Aquaculture 2019 (LAQUA19)

    "Zoning of the Pacific coast of Costa Rica based on chlorophyll content for macroalgae mariculture" (pag.341 Abstract Book). As a first approach for a better use of Costa Rican ocean resources, it was identified and characterized high productive coast zones using Geographic Information Systems (GIS)
